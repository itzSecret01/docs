## 🇺🇸 How to Create Your Server on CreeperCloud

To get started with your FiveM server, follow these steps on [CreeperCloud](https://creepercloud.io/):

## Step 1: Access the Website

- Go to [CreeperCloud.io](https://creepercloud.io/)    

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-05-16171254.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-05-16 171254.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-05-16 171254.png"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

- Log into your account or register if you haven't yet.

- Now you will be redirected in to [deploy.creepercloud.io](https://deploy.creepercloud.io/) (this is the deploy site where you create servers etc...) 

## Step 2: Create Your Server

In the panel, click **"Create Server"** 

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-05-16175310.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-05-16 175310.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-05-16 175310.png"
  style={{ width:"43%" }}
/>

- Now fill in the form with the following (recommended) :

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-05-16180247.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-05-16 180247.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-05-16 180247.png"
  style={{ width:"80%" }}
/>

| Field              | Example Value       | Notes                            |

|-------------------|---------------------|----------------------------------|

| **Name**          | `MyFiveMServer`     | Choose a recognizable or custom name.      |

| **CPU (%)**      | `100` (100% recommended) | You have up to ---% available (depending of your plan). |

| **Memory (RAM)**  | `8192` MB           | Recommended based on players.    |

| **Disk (Storage)**| `60000` MB          | Allocate enough space.           |

| **Additional Ports** | `0`              | Optional unless needed.          |

| **Backups**       | `0`                 | Configure if you want backups. |

### Step 3: CPU, RAM and Storage Recommendations

Here’s what we recommend depending on the number of players:

| Players     | CPU (%) | RAM (MB) | Disk (MB) |

|-------------|---------|----------|-----------|

| 0–20        | 100–200 | 2048–4096 | 30000\+    |

| 20–50       | 200–400 | 4096–8192 | 50000\+    |

| 50–100\+     | 400–800 | 8192–16000 | 70000\+   |

\> ⚠️**Always allocate at maximum 400% CPU for FiveM servers ** (and other types of servers), and \
**at least 100%** even for small projects. (the top cpu can change at anytime in terms\! )

## 🇪🇸 Cómo crear tu servidor en CreeperCloud

Para comenzar con tu servidor de FiveM, sigue estos pasos en [CreeperCloud](https://creepercloud.io/):

### Paso 1: Entra a la web

- Visita [CreeperCloud.io](https://creepercloud.io/)

  ![Screenshot 2025-05-16 171254.png](/images/Screenshot2025-05-16171254.png)

  - Inicia sesión o regístrate si no tienes cuenta.

  - Ahora serás redirigido a [deploy.creepercloud.io](http://deploy.creepercloud.io) (este es el sitio es donde creas servidores, etc.) 

### Paso 2: Crear tu servidor

En el panel, haz clic en **"Create Server"**  

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-05-16175310.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2025-05-16 175310.png"
  title="Screenshot 2025-05-16 175310.png"
  style={{ width:"40%" }}
/>

- Rellena el formulario con lo siguiente (recomendado):

![Screenshot 2025-05-16 181903.png](/images/Screenshot2025-05-16181903.png)

| Campos              | Valor de ejemplo     | Comentarios                          |

|--------------------|----------------------|-------------------------------------|

| **Nombre**         | `MiServidordeFiveM`    | Usa un nombre reconocible o personalizado.          |

| **CPU (%)**   | `100` (recomendado 100%)  | Tienes hasta ---% disponible (dependiendo de tu plan).     |

| **Memoria (RAM)**  | `8192` MB            | Según número de jugadores.          |

| **Disco (Almacenamiento)** | `60000` MB | Asegúrate de tener espacio suficiente. |

| **Puertos adicionales** | `0`            | Opcional si no se requieren extras. |

| **Copias de seguridad** | `0`            | Puedes configurar tus copias de seguridad si deseas.|

### Paso 3: Recomendaciones para CPU, RAM y almacenamiento

Según la cantidad de jugadores, recomendamos lo siguiente:

| Jugadores   | CPU (%) | RAM (MB) | Disco (MB) |

|-------------|---------|----------|------------|

| 0–20        | 100–200 | 2048–4096 | 30000\+     |

| 20–50       | 200–400 | 4096–8192 | 50000\+     |

| 50–100\+     | 400–800 | 8192–16000 | 70000\+    |

\> ⚠️ **Siempre debes asignar como maximo un 400% de CPU a tu servidor FiveM **(y otros tipos de servidores),  y incluso si es solo para pruebas **al menos** un **100%.**
